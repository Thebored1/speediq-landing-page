             templates, segments, live inbox, and analytics across WhatsApp,

====================================================
CHANNELS
====================================================

- WhatsApp  (templates, broadcasts, live chat via Meta Business API)
- Email     (campaigns, custom domain, drag-and-drop HTML builder)
- SMS/10DLC (US/Canada + global, OTPs, transactional alerts — Twilio-powered)
- Calls     (listed as coming soon)


====================================================
KEY PLATFORM FEATURES
====================================================

1. Campaigns
   - Schedule broadcasts with live progress tracking
   - Per-channel campaign editors (WhatsApp, Email, SMS)
   - Campaign cancellation and retry of failed messages
   - Send to single contact or bulk audience

2. Templates
   - WhatsApp: build and submit templates to Meta with auto-approval syncing
     - Refresh approval status on demand
     - View submission logs
     - Test send to verify before submission
     - Media support: images, videos, documents in headers
   - Email: HTML drag-and-drop builder with variable substitution
     (first_name, last_name, email, and custom fields)
   - SMS: plain-text templates

3. Segments (Audience Segmentation)
   - Reusable audience filters with AND/OR logic (WhatsApp)
   - Real-time audience size estimation (audience count endpoint)
   - Contact tagging (tag definitions per project)

4. Inbox (Live Chat / Conversations)
   - Unified reply management across WhatsApp and SMS
   - Conversation assignment to team members
   - Auto-merging of threads
   - Quick replies (saved response shortcuts)
   - Canned messages with media attachments (images, etc.)
   - Mark conversations as read
   - Media viewing within conversations

5. Automations
   - Drag-and-drop workflow builder
   - Triggers, branching logic, and webhooks
   - Inbound handling for email replies

6. Analytics
   - Delivery, open, click, and reply rate tracking
   - Per-channel analytics (WhatsApp, Email, SMS)
   - Retention: 30 days (Starter), 90 days (Pro), 1 year (Business)
   - Usage event logging

7. Team & Access Control
   - Multi-seat management
   - Four role levels per project: Owner, Admin, Editor, Viewer
   - Team member invitations via email (with token-based acceptance/decline)
   - Member removal

8. Contacts
   - Unified contact list across channels
   - Bulk CSV import with custom field mapping
   - Contact profile viewing
   - Bulk update
   - Tag-based segmentation (WhatsApp)
   - Contact-level conversation history

9. Settings
   - WhatsApp account configuration (WABA via Meta Embedded Signup)
   - Email provider settings (Resend or SMTP; custom domain verification)
   - SMS settings (Twilio OAuth)
   - Tag definitions management
   - Notification preferences (per user)

10. Billing
    - Subscription management (Starter, Pro, Business)
    - Credit top-ups
    - Auto-recharge at configurable thresholds
    - Billing portal (Stripe)
    - Cancel subscription

11. AI Assist
    - AI-powered message generation
    - Cost: 10 credits per generation
    - Available on Business plan

====================================================
ONBOARDING / SETUP FLOW
====================================================

Three-step process:
  1. Connect channels
     - WhatsApp: Meta Embedded Signup (user retains WABA ownership)
     - Email:    Domain verification via Resend or SMTP config
     - SMS:      Twilio OAuth connection + 10DLC registration (US/Canada)
  2. Add contacts (CSV import with custom field mapping)
  3. Send / reply / automate

Authentication options:
  - Sign in with Google (OAuth)
  - Email + password
